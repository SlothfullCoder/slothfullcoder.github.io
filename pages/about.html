About Vavenby

Vavenby is a small, picturesque town located in British Columbia, Canada. Known for its stunning natural landscapes and friendly community, Vavenby offers a unique blend of outdoor adventures and local charm.

Whether you're exploring the nearby forests, enjoying local events, or visiting our businesses, Vavenby has something for everyone.
