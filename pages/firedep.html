Volunteer Opportunities

Interested in joining? The department welcomes new volunteers. Training is provided for all members.

Fire Safety Tips

1. Install smoke detectors in every room.

2. Create and practice a fire escape plan.

3. Keep flammable materials away from heat sources.

4. Never leave cooking unattended.

5. Use candles safely and keep them away from flammable items.
